Did you know?


If, under the "Choose what closing the lid does" options, you choose to not let your laptop go to sleep when you close the lid, it does not lock your Windows user account.

Instead it justs turns off the display and opens up exactly the way you left it before you shut the lid down, this is a major security issue that needs to be taken care of. Enter LidLock!

Instructions
Step 1: Control Panel > Power Options > Choose what closing the lid does > When I close the lid > "Do nothing"

Step 2: Download the executable

If you want the program to run at StartUp

Step 3: Right click at LidLock.exe > Create Shortcut > save it to a folder or to your desktop > right click at the shortcut > click Copy.

Open Run > at the Run box, type %AppData% > click OK .

You are now on Roaming window > right side, click open Microsoft > scroll down and open Windows > open Start Menu > open Programs > open Startup

you are now at Startup window > on right side, right click at an open spot > click Paste > LidLock is now added to the Startup.
